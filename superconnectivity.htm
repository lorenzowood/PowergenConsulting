Super connectivity

Super connectivity is an approach to “smart” working for groups and teams. It is a framework of principles that can be used both as a diagnostic and as planning aid for new initiatives.

The principles are based around two types of connections:

  • Predictable connections are those that we make intentionally. These include searching for information; receiving updates of information relevant to our work; communicating within our teams and with other individuals (in meetings and remotely); collaborating on work.
  • Unpredictable connections are those that happen by chance. These include lateral thinking; ad hoc involvement in work; coming upon something valuable from simply browsing. Unpredictable connections are the powerhouse of innovation, and often a source of real insight.
